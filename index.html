/ 01 — MISSION

A Leader in Youth-Led
Global Impact

UNbound is a virtual student-led leadership network connecting English-speaking schools around the world. We equip students with the tools, guidance, and a global lens to lead community-based service initiatives—sparking lasting impact that begins in schools and ripples outward.

We challenge the traditional, silo-centric "club" model by cultivating multi-stakeholder, SDG-aligned action plans that elevate student agency and transform extracurricular learning into something far more ambitious.

/ 03 — ORIGIN

Why we started this:
An OpSmile Story

The moment behind the mission

At 16, as I stood in the recovery room of an Operation Smile medical mission—translating between surgeons and nurses and playing with children before life-changing cleft palate surgeries—I had an idea that would change everything. As Finance Chair of Miles for Smiles (M4S), the largest student-led Operation Smile initiative in Mexico, I rallied over 1,000 runners each year for the M4S charity race and helped raise $30,000 annually— enough for around 20 surgeries.

But I kept seeing a bigger pattern. While powerful, initiatives like M4S often operated in silos. So I asked myself: What if students everywhere could build something of their own... together?

"Miles for Smiles is a reminder of what we can do when we come together for something bigger than ourselves."

— Lukah Villarreal, UNbound Youth Initiative Founder

/ 05 — GUEST SPEAKERS

Learning from real changemakers

UNbound gives students a seat at the table with people who are actually changing the world. Our guest speakers share their stories, inspire deep reflection, and show what it looks like to turn conviction into action.

Anton Jongeneel

UNITED STATES REP · UN SECURITY COUNCIL

Anton Jongeneel is a Columbia Law School graduate currently serving as a U.S. Political Advisor in the UN Security Council, where he leads strategy on the Ukraine conflict. Over a 13-year State Department career, he has served in places like the  DRC and Afghanistan. At UNbound, Anton will reframe everyday empathy as a catalyst of global change.

Brendan Iribe

CO-FOUNDER · OCULUS VR & FORMER CEO

After redefining virtual reality with Oculus—which sold to Facebook for $2 billion in 2014—Brendan has worked on building Sesame, an AI wearable designed to make artificial intelligence physical and ever-present. In his Fellowship conference, Brendan will challenge Fellows to explore innovation as an inevitable byproduct of understanding and iteration.

Seung Paik

RETIRED COLONEL · U.S. AIR FORCE

While teaching at the U.S. Air Force Academy, Seung created Leadernomics, a framework that has spent 20 years reaching leaders across government, the private sector, and academia. After 25 years of military service and a career of award-winning leadership, Seung strives to transform with his core belief in the power of "what is" and "what should be."
